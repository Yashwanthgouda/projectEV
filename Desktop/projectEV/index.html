About ProjectEV

ProjectEV is designed to revolutionize electric vehicle (EV) charging by optimizing the entire process for efficiency and cost-effectiveness. Our platform helps EV owners locate the most energy-efficient charging stations based on their current battery levels and travel routes.

Key features include real-time station availability, dynamic pricing suggestions based on off-peak hours, and slot booking functionality to avoid wait times. Our goal is to make EV charging smarter, reducing both costs and environmental impact.

Join us in driving towards a sustainable future, where technology and innovation lead the way in transforming energy management for electric vehicles.
